About NITROCOOL

A personal engineering project focused on rapid, practical cooling for high-heat performance and safety.

Why NITROCOOL exists

Heat limits output and increases risk—whether you’re training, working outdoors, or responding on scene. Many existing solutions are slow, inconvenient, non-portable, or impractical during active use.

NITROCOOL is a response to that gap: instant, battery-free cooling in a compact system designed for real workflows—grounded in mechanical engineering and thermodynamics (controlled CO₂ expansion cooling).

Mission

Build a cooling system that’s fast, portable, and credible—grounded in thermodynamics and validated through testing.
