Research & validation

Early-stage projects earn trust with a clear plan. This page outlines how NITROCOOL intends to validate cooling performance, usability, and safety.

Planned testing (near term)

  • Temperature mapping: IR camera + contact sensors to measure skin temperature delta.
  • Cooling duration: time-to-cool and time-to-return-to-baseline under repeatable conditions.
  • Usability: activation time, ergonomics, and field handling.
  • Comfort: perceived cooling, skin comfort, and safe exposure parameters.

Human performance focus (longer term)

As validation progresses, NITROCOOL aims to explore protocols that matter to real users: recovery between efforts, perceived exertion, and decision performance in high heat.

Collaboration goals

NITROCOOL is open to collaboration with:

  • Universities and engineering labs
  • Human performance and athletic programs
  • Safety departments and field organizations

If/when human-subject studies are pursued, appropriate oversight (e.g., IRB) will be followed.
